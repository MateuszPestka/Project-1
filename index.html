What we do

We at Vegan Drop Meals provide you with an easy way to get the right nutrients your body requires while maintaining an 100% vegan diet. Not only do you save time by not having to cook meals after a hard stressful day at work you just simply get them delivered to you everyday. You no longer have to spend time thinking about how to get the right nutrients in when they're waiting at your doorstep.

How it works

Vegan Drop Meals is incredibly simple. Once you decide on what package you'd like to go with you simply click on the package below and it will bring you to the contact us section. You can also access it yourself from the top of the website. You then fill in your contact details and your contact preference of choice and one of our dieticians will contact you to discuss diet requirements. You then just have to wait for the food to arrive at your doorstep.

Ingredients we use

At Vegan Drop Meals we aim to provide the best nutrition and for that we used the best quality products. Our vegetables are from locally sourced farms and we use 100% locally grown produce. We cover Galway, Dublin and Cork areas with sources in each county.

Speak to our dieticians

If you've made up your mind or simply would like to know more about what we do feel free to go over to the contact us page to speak with one of our dieticians or go over to our social media accounts where you can see the process of the food being made and the farm to table process that happens. You can also click here or one of the packages below and it will lead to the contact us page.

Just the ingredients

This package is perfect for anyone who loves to cook but hates to shop. We provide you with the ingredients and cooking instructions but what you do with them is completly up to you!

Breakfast, lunch and dinner

If you struggle to find the time to fit balanced meals into your diet we have you covered with this package. This package not only includes all your meals for the entire week but they are cooked and all you have to do is follow the reheating instructions.

Weekday only

Don't have energy to cook during the week? Well with this package you'll get more time for yourself and will have healthy meals without the time needed to cook them. You will recieve cooked meals Monday-Friday at your doorstep.

Exclusive

The VIP package allows you to place orders for meals. Not only do you get meals delivered to you doorstep you get to choose exactly what you want each day from our long list of meals. You can also add to the list by talking to our specialists.
